At Clementine's Pet Spa & Grooming, we are proud to provide the best pet grooming services for pets of all breeds and sizes. As an award-winning pet spa, our experienced team is dedicated to making every visit comfortable, relaxing, and enjoyable while helping your pet look and feel their very best. Our welcoming environment is designed to reduce stress and ensure every pet receives the attention, comfort, and care they deserve from the moment they arrive.

Our professional cat and dog grooming services include bathing, brushing, nail trimming, ear cleaning, and coat care tailored to your pet's individual needs. We use gentle handling techniques and quality grooming products to ensure every pet receives safe and personalized care.

As a top rated pet spa, we believe every pet deserves patience, kindness, and exceptional care. Whether you're visiting us for the first time or returning for regular grooming, we look forward to welcoming you and providing an experience both you and your pet can trust.
