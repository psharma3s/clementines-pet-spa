Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I schedule my appointment?

We recommend scheduling your appointment at least one week in advance to secure your preferred date and time. During busy seasons or holidays, booking earlier is encouraged to ensure availability.

Can I request a specific groomer?

Yes! If your preferred groomer is available, we'll do our best to accommodate your request. Our experienced grooming team works together to provide the same high level of care for every pet.

How long will my pet's appointment take?

Most grooming appointments take between one and three hours, depending on your pet's size, coat condition, temperament, and the services requested. We'll provide an estimated completion time when confirming your appointment.

What should I bring to my appointment?

Please let us know about any allergies, medical conditions, or special grooming requests before your visit. Sharing this information helps us provide the safest and most comfortable experience for your pet.

What if I need to cancel or reschedule?

If your plans change and need to reschedule your pet grooming appointment, we kindly ask that you notify us at least 24 hours in advance. This allows us to offer the appointment to another pet while helping us maintain an efficient schedule.
